Bio2Vec

Smart data analytics for life sciences.

Bio2Vec aims to develop the methods and infrastructure that allows users to integrate and analyze large-scale heterogenous life science data. Bio2Vec will generate embeddings from text and knowledge graphs to learn representations of proteins, drugs, phenotypes, diseases, and interactions. Bio2Vec will also provide a repository of FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable) data and models.
